FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Foster Brains programs and services.

How do the online classes work?

Our classes are conducted via video conferencing platforms. Once enrolled, you'll schedule sessions with your teacher. Each session lasts 45–60 minutes and is fully interactive.

What age groups do you serve?

We offer programs for ages 7–18: Young Learners (7–9), Smart Scholar (10–14), and Teen Skill Pathway (15–18). Our main English and tutoring programs focus on ages 9–14.

Are classes 1-on-1 or group sessions?

We offer both! 1-on-1 sessions for maximum individual attention, and group sessions for students who benefit from peer interaction. You choose based on your child's learning style.

How much do sessions cost?

Tutoring and English communication classes start at $18.99 per session. Psychological counseling is $21.99. International batch tutoring starts as low as $12.99/hour. Contact us for package deals.

Do you offer flexible scheduling?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to accommodate different time zones and busy family routines. We work around your schedule.

Is the counseling service confidential?

Yes, completely. All psychological counseling sessions are strictly confidential. Our psychologists follow professional ethical standards ensuring your child's privacy and comfort at all times.

How do I get started?

Simply fill in the contact form below, or reach us via WhatsApp or email. We'll schedule a free consultation to understand your child's needs and recommend the best program.

What subjects do you teach?

We specialize in English (reading, writing, speaking, vocabulary, grammar), Science taught in English, Math, and Communication Skills—plus comprehensive child psychological counseling.
